Rodent Control

Eliminate Rodents — And Keep Them Out

Mice and rats are more than a nuisance. They chew through wiring (a leading cause of house fires), contaminate food with droppings and urine, and can spread serious diseases including hantavirus, salmonella, and leptospirosis. A single pair of mice can produce up to 60 offspring in a year.

Partners Pest Solutions takes a comprehensive approach: we don't just set traps and leave. We identify and seal entry points, remove harborage conditions, and establish control measures that deliver lasting results.

Rodents We Control

  • House mouse (Mus musculus)
  • Norway rat / brown rat (Rattus norvegicus)
  • Roof rat / black rat (Rattus rattus)
  • Deer mouse and field mice

Signs of Infestation

Droppings along baseboards, gnaw marks on food packaging or wiring, grease rub marks, nesting material (shredded paper/fabric), scratching sounds at night.

Entry Points

Mice can squeeze through a gap the size of a dime. Common entry points include gaps around pipes, vents, doors, windows, and foundation cracks.

Our Methods

Physical exclusion (sealing entry points), snap traps, glue boards, interior and exterior bait stations, and sanitation recommendations.

Commercial Service

Rodent control programs for restaurants, food processing, warehouses, and multi-unit properties with full documentation and compliance logs.

The Exclusion Difference

Most pest companies trap rodents and call it done. The problem is that if the entry points aren't sealed, new rodents will simply move in to fill the void. Our exclusion work identifies and seals the gaps, cracks, and penetrations that allow rodents access — addressing the root cause rather than just symptoms.

Ongoing Monitoring Programs

For commercial properties or homes with recurring pressure, we install and maintain a network of exterior bait stations and interior monitors that provide continuous control and early detection. Regular service visits include inspection reports documenting activity levels and actions taken.
