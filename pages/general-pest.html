General Pest Control

Comprehensive Protection for Your Home or Business

Common household pests like ants, cockroaches, spiders, and silverfish aren't just a nuisance — they can contaminate food, damage property, and trigger allergies. Partners Pest Solutions offers targeted, effective treatment plans that eliminate active infestations and create a barrier against future intrusions.

We use Integrated Pest Management (IPM) principles, combining inspection, targeted treatments, and preventative recommendations to address the root cause — not just the symptoms.

Pests We Treat

  • Ants (including fire ants and carpenter ants)
  • Cockroaches (American, German, Oriental)
  • Spiders (including brown recluse and black widow)
  • Silverfish and firebrats
  • Earwigs and centipedes
  • Stinging insects (wasps, hornets, yellow jackets)
  • Bed bugs
  • Fleas and ticks (interior treatments)

Common Signs

Droppings, shed skins, damage to food packaging, visible insects, musty odors, or egg cases in dark corners.

Treatment Methods

Liquid perimeter spray, baiting stations, crack-and-crevice applications, dusting, and exclusion sealing.

Service Frequency

One-time treatments available. Monthly, bi-monthly, and quarterly maintenance plans recommended for lasting control.

Safe for Families

All products applied by licensed technicians. We follow all label requirements and re-entry intervals.

Our Process

Every service starts with a thorough inspection of your property — interior and exterior. We identify pest activity, entry points, and conducive conditions. From there we build a treatment plan specific to your situation, not a one-size-fits-all spray.

After each visit, your technician will walk you through what was done, what was found, and any recommendations to reduce risk going forward.

Residential & Commercial

We serve single-family homes, multi-unit properties, restaurants, offices, warehouses, and retail spaces. Commercial clients receive documentation for compliance records including pesticide application logs.
